\levelstay{Coupling} \label{sec:qubits:coupling}

In this section we discuss qubit coupling.

\leveldown{Capacitive coupling}

\begin{equation}
H_g = \frac{\hbar}{2} \frac{C_g}{\sqrt{C_1 C_2}} \sqrt{\omega_1 \omega_2} \, .
\end{equation}

\levelstay{Inductive coupling}

\begin{equation}
H_g \stackrel{?}{=} \frac{\hbar}{2} \frac{M}{L_1 L_2} \Phi_1 \Phi_2 \, .
\end{equation}
